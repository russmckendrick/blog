A short, fast Docker introduction co-written with Pethuru Raj, Jeeva S. Chelladhurai, and Vinod Singh. The format is built around a seven-day plan, each day covering a different slice of Docker: installation, images, networks, storage, Compose, Swarm, and common troubleshooting.

Aimed at developers and IT professionals who want a working knowledge of Docker without committing to a longer reference. The book is no longer in print, so links are not provided.
