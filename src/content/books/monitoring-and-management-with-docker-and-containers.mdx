A free ebook from The New Stack on monitoring and managing containerised systems. The wider book gathers perspectives from several practitioners on the operational side of running containers in production, with a focus on the tooling shifts that came with the move to service-oriented architectures.

I contributed a chapter called *Identifying And Collecting Container Data*, covering how to extract metrics from Docker containers and the hosts they run on, and what to do with those metrics once you have them.
