The second edition of The Kubernetes Bible, co-written with Gineesh Madapparambath. Updates the first edition's cluster, networking, and deployment material to match Kubernetes' current state, with wider security coverage including Immutable ConfigMaps, RBAC patterns, and operator-driven security.

The cluster management chapters cover GKE, EKS, and AKS as before, alongside on-premises deployments. Suitable for readers who already know Kubernetes basics and want a deeper, current reference.
